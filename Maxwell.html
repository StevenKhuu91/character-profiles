The Way of Dance - Utilize dexterous movements to confuse and attack. Allows the stacking of 'Primed' upon successful hits. Executing skills under the Primed state enhances their effects. Stacks up to 3 times.
The military forces of Er Vereldia are trained in dance before they are ever given a weapon to swing. Maxwell was no exception to this, and as such he was able to gain a strong foundation of how his body moved and worked as a whole. Light on his feet and swifter than most of his party members, he's able to utilize his entire weight behind every attack thanks to his blinding agility. Maxwell is able to deftly dodge most attacks as well with ease.
Sparrow Strike - Run the enemy through with a slash/stab at blinding speed. Utilizing this while Primed allows a second Sparrow Strike to be executed right after the first.
The first arte that Maxwell ever learned upon swinging a sword. As such, it has been perfected to the point of being his most used ability. Maxwell is able to redirect himself in midair before the second activation of Sparrow Strike, allowing for a maneuver that employs both offense and defense all at once. Excessive usage however can lead to some serious whiplash.
Air Hike - Create a burst of wind beneath you, powering a secondary jump to reach greater heights. Utilizing this while Primed allows the usage of 'Air Ride', which allows you to swiftly soar upon the winds briefly.
