Liam Ranvir
The Xenoglossy

About -

A once leader of his tried-and-true friends in the Queensguard.
Now on a quest of self-discovery and adventure for himself.
Wanderlust hangs heavy over his mind, but the encounters and stories he'll write along the way will be all the worthwhile for it.
The tinge of regret and sadness however looms ever closer, as a constant reminder of his failures.

Current Pursuits -

Destination: Unknown.
Passerby encounters and fleeting instances have yet to hold major import, yet.

The Xenoglossy -

The title of Liam's combat occupation.
The Xenoglossy imbues both light & dark variations of elemental magicks into their weapons.
This strength is derived from their memories and connections with individuals.

& spells

xenoglossy artes

spell passive All basic elemental spells take on properties based on which alignment Liam has swapped to. Light will typically spread and provide AoE traits to magic, while Darkness converges magic into focused points of deadly precision.

elemental arsenal

spell passive Fire, Water, Wind, & Earth magicks heed Liam's call. While he holds no major mastery over any of them, the versatility combined with his Xenoglossy talents acts as a formidable toolkit to deal with a variety of encounters and situations.

chain of memories

spell active The Mark of the Xenoglossy empowers its bearer with the memories and connections they've made. Liam is capable of actively switching his 'Chain of Memories' to specific individuals of import in his life, thereby strenghtening his magicks and oftentimes providing him with new abilities in turn. The effects of the current Chain will remain until swapped.

vermillion flare

spell active Combining all elemental magicks within his arsenal, Liam unleashes a chaotic storm of focused fury upon an enemy, scorching and rendering them asunder. Usable only under the most dire of conditions and circumstances, this will leave him drained for a significant amount of time afterward.

blade logic

skill passive Liam adopts a duelistic style of combat most of the time, though he can handle himself just as well when the odds are stacked against him. Among his preferred weapons, he employs the use of swords and spears the most.

blade logic: rend

skill active The first branch of his teachings, and the objectively the most powerful given his constant usage of it. Liam attacks with his aligned elements, and sends shockwaves from his weapon that burst forth from the point of impact. Particularly useful for armored foes.

blade logic: sever

skill active The second branch of blade logic. Liam can send projectiles from his weapon based on his chosen elemental alignment. The effects vary dramatically between one another.

inventory.

fehlgrace.

Refined, reforged, and remade countless times. A lightweight bastard sword that has stood the test of time and tribulations. The unique meteoric alloy it was crafted from provides it with the astounding trait to hold its edge almost indefinitely. It becomes even deadlier once imbued with Liam's magicks as a Xenoglossy.

a saint's ring.

A promise made tangible. Often worn in the form of a necklace around Liam's neck. It bears qualities similar to a communication device, and allows him to speak to Saint at certain moments.

traveller's pack.

A curious bag that can disappear and reappear on command whenever Liam wills it. Otherwise, it's used normally like any other pack. A steadfast traveling companion for any adventurer.
