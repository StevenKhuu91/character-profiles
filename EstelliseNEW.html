Bread!
Bread is a staple among many cultures in various forms, and any baker worth their salt is capable of making specific kinds of bread for different purposes. Estellise is well-versed in crafting various types ranging from croissants, ciabatta, and even challah.

Caramel Flan!
A personal favorite of Estellise. While simple and easy to make, it's hard to master and perfect. It isn't a pastry per say, but was her first sweet dish that she made for her parents to try. It has a special nostalgia that's dear to her heart.

Chocolate Cake Log with Vanilla Creme!
One of the first things Estellise was assigned to make during her beginning years as a pastry chef. Simple in appearance, a cake log requires plenty of planning ahead and preparation to put together properly. Now, this dish is a mastered recipe in the young chef's repertoire.

Gift Cake!
Gift Cakes are a specialty of Estellise at the Starry Sky Bakery. These come in various flavors and colors, but their signature look is cube-shaped with a fondant ribbon over it. Given their versatile purpose for any special occassion, these remain one of the best sellers at the bakery.

Meat Pie Bun!
Meat pie buns are an excellent way to use leftover ground beef, as they require little preparation and can be made quickly. Estellise is often seen eating one of these, with a new curry sauce she's been concocting as of late. Suffice to say, it makes for an easy but delicious savory lunch.

Taiyaki!
A traditional Japanese pastry made with a crisp waffle-like outside in the shape of a fish and filled with sweet red beans. Another popular choice at Starry Sky, Estellise delights in figuring out what sort of new fillings she can come up with for seasonal flavors. Sometimes she'll even use different shapes.

Fruit Jelly!
A random creation utilizing the leftover fruits that didn't make it into her pastries. These were originally a test run at first, served with a side of homemade whipped cream. Customers however loved them, especially when they were chilled during the Summer. They're now a Spring & Summer seasonal item on the bakery's menu.

Macaron!
A notoriously difficult French pastry to make properly. Estellise has spent hours attempting to perfect her craft with this, and has had excellent results as of late. Given the multitude of combinations of flavors with both the meringue and filling, these remain a staple side item at Starry Sky.

Fengli Su!
Otherwise known more commonly as 'Pineapple Cakes'. These originated in China, but are found all over the Asia now as a popular treat. Crumbly shortcrust pastry surrounds pineapple jam filling, which pairs extremely well with unsweetened tea! These go on sale during Summer, when the imported pineapples are at their sweetest.

Special Strawberry SUPREME!
A ridiculous food challenge exclusive to Starry Sky. Estellise LOVES to go over the top when making this. This beast of a parfait starts with strawberry jam between two slices of cake, a hefty middle section of frozen fruit custard, and topped with strawberry ice cream, whipped cream, a chunk of white chocolate, & strawberry pocky with even MORE strawberry jam drizzled over everything.

Born into a loving family, Estellise was given humble beginnings, though she learned to make the most out of everything she had. Being the only child, one might have believed the expectations of her parents to be heavy on her shoulders, yet a glimpse into her life would prove otherwise; Estellise was raised to pursue her own goals and dreams, with her parents only wishing for her to be happy in life. Unfortunately, things would change drastically when a drunk driver turned the girl's world upside-down, as she'd receive news one night of her parents caught up in a fatal accident.

For some few years Estellise grieved, unsure of how to go about her life with such sudden circumstances hoisted upon her out of nowhere. In the end, she was able to lift herself back up thanks to both friends and extended family, the memory of her parents living on in her heart as she tries to live the life they wanted for her.
