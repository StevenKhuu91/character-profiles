Current Events

With plans to separate 'The Syndicate' from its bloody history, Mattimeo attempts to lead his organization towards a more sustainable future. However many of the older members who were a part of his adoptive father's era (otherwise known as Old Bloods) disagree with the direction The Syndicate is taking, saying that Mattimeo is still too young and inexperienced to make such a decision for everyone. As such, he is wrongfully accused of civil homicide and betraying his values, no doubt framed by the Old Bloods. Fortunately for Mattimeo, he is not removed wholly from The Syndicate, but rather demoted to the lowest rank of Messenger thanks to those still loyal to him and the memory of Ravus.

Mattimeo now works as a Messenger, rediscovering the city he grew up in and finding new things about it every day, along with the re-establishing of old friendships he'd lost along the way, or perhaps realize he'd never quite lost them at all to begin with.

History

→ Orphaned. Parents abandoned him for unknown reasons to an orphanage.
→ Wrongfully labeled as a 'trouble child' early on in his life.
→ Strangely studious for his age. Found a passion for reading.
→ Had little interest in wanting to be adopted, thus remained in the orphanage for most of his younger life.
→ Came into a rebellious streak as a teenager. Struggled with his identity due to past stereotypes.
→ Seemingly a natural-born leader. Often attracted people to him without realizing it.
→ Despite being troublesome, he went on to graduate early at the age of 17.
→ Was adopted sometime later just before adulthood by a man named Ravus Bizeveron, the then Chief Director of 'The Syndicate'.
→ Joined 'The Syndicate' not long after his adoption. Made an official member within days.
→ Rose up through the ranks swiftly by his own right, though being the Chief Director's son made some difference.
→ Found himself becoming more of a progressive thinker, with the charisma to follow it allowing for leadership.
→ Eventually replaced Ravus as Chief Director, after his adoptive father's untimely death.
→ Originally a crime gang with origins tracing back towards last two centuries.
→ Extremely organized. Sense of code and conduct among members. #1 Rule: No harming civilians.
→ Profits include: money laundering, illegal drugs, mercenary work.
→ Formerly headed by Ravus Bezeveron. Death was from health complications.
→ Hierarchy is as follows: Messenger ► Sworn ► Officer ► Branch Captain (of 3) ► Chief Director
Anira

The House Pet

A childhood friend of Mattimeo's. The two had become separated over the years, with their occupations and lives forcing them apart for some time. It wasn't until recently he came into contact with Ani again, to which she explained her plight and current circumstances. Suffice to say, he was eager to assist, though in the end their mutual feelings overtook their senses.

After breaking her in, Ani opted to stay with 'Meo' as she enjoys referring to him as. Having awakened her more sultry and perverse nature, she now resides with him at his home, ever still the loyal friend but also as his official 'House Pet'.

Luxanna

Lieutenant

A woman of class and prestige in her line, Luxanna eventually came to Mattimeo's side during his time as Chief Director in the organization. Drawn to him by his capability to lead, the two were gradually drawn to one another through personal experiences and the like.

After the events of the fallout between Mattimeo and the Old Bloods, Luxanna looked to remove herself from her position of influence, in order to stay at the man's side in more ways than one. In turn, feelings were left unrestrained as emotions ran high, and after a fateful night he opted to claim Luxanna as his.

Eilís

Elven Sweetheart

Mattimeo's myriad of connections brought him to encounter Eilis, who he affectionately refers to as 'El'. Once an escort, some few chance encounters after the man's supposed fall from grace brought the two closer, extending their relationship beyond a professional level.

Given the social status of elves in their world, Mattimeo by legal rights 'owns' El, though he hardly treats her like such. Instead, he's been shown to be rather sweet on the girl, though at its core their friendly demeanor with one another has hardly changed. If anything, things have deepened to a degree, though he hopes to prove to her his word about keeping her from harm's way.
