Prologue

Once a childhood leader among his merry band of friends, now an outcast thanks to adulthood's harsh realities. A promise to remain together, to stay as a family, amounting to nothing in the eyes of those with depraved greed. Dreams of adventuring became illusions of grandeur, and as a result a once hopeful young man became lost and sent adrift to fend for himself. Years later he finds himself a hired Ranger of his home nation, a farcry from what he originally envisioned for himself, but it's a stable job that keeps his wits and instincts steady, in the hopes that one day he might embark on a true adventure worth writing... one he can chronicle for himself.

Lightweight, reliable, and made of the strongest steel available to Liam. The unique scabbard allows for quick unsheathing and sheathing action. Havoc - Hand Axe

Custom-made for Liam. The broad axe head with unintrusive beard allows for the maneuverability of the hand and wrist with minimal interference, while still retaining the ability to effectively hook onto things (and people). The ring pommel has a leather strap tied to it, which is used essentially as a fulcrum point to land devastating blows. Additionally, it chops wood decently.
