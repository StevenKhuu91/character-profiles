these memories.

painted on glass, yet unbreakable.

The 'Red String', otherwise known as the concept of 'Soulmates' forever bound to one another in every life, was in fact a magic that a certain young man had turned to in order to fulfill the wishes of his dying lover. Through each and every incarnation of their lives, they would come to discover their past memories as they came of age, a perpetual cycle that would lead them back to one another each and every time no matter where in the world they were. However, through many lifetimes they began to feel that 'Red String' weakening, their memories becoming distorted and overlapping with their past lives.

It wasn't until Verdachiel, or rather 'Vincent' as he went by now, had taken things into his own hands in order to free his partner from the painful inevitability of their minds succumbing to the cruel sands of times. Seeking out the once long-gone magic at its source, he traveled with his lover to see her off for one final time, to tell her that even should she forget him that he would always love and cherish the moments they spent together. The irreversible process of 'severance' cut the 'Red String' binding their souls, and in the process wiping away any and all memories his significant other had of him and their time together.

While his memories remain intact and unblurred, Vincent finds the reminiscence and nostalgia of his lost life to be too much, resulting in him moving away in an attempt to start over anew. For better or worse, he is freed from the cycle, his 'last' life made painfully evident before him now. Only time can tell what he'll eventually find, to discover the renewed purpose of his existence now.
