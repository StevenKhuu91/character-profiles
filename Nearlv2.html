History

Margaret Nearl, granddaughter of Kazimierz's national hero Kirill Nearl, was once the candidate for Knight Primus in the Nearl family to succeed her grandfather. However her vocal opposition towards the rampant commercialization of the Kazimierz Major tournaments led her to desert the Adeptus Sprawiedliwi Knights and join the Major in an attempt to change the country from within. Once Margaret became a prominent MVP within the tournaments, the Major's stakeholders, primarily known as the General Chamber of Commerce, made plans to remove the Radiant Knight permanently from the scene using the Armorless Legion.

As a result, Kirill announced that Margaret was an 'Infected', one of the many stricken with the incurable disease known as Oripathy, and sentenced her to exile in order to protect her from assassination. Forced to leave her family behind, Margaret obeyed while being unaware of her condition, treating her exile as training for herself. It is eventually revealed that the 'infection' was a fabrication, though by the time news of such reached the general populace Kirill Nearl had passed away.

It was during her exile that she became stronger than ever before, encountering two wandering Sarkaz healers known as Shining and Nightingale, to which they'd form the group known as 'The Followers', a trio dedicated to providing healing and treatment to all regardless of being Infected or not. Eventually this caught the eye of Rhodes Island, leading Margaret to join with the fateful crew that would ultimately pave the path of redemption to Kazimierz.

Upon her return to Kazimierz and dominating the 24th Major season, Margaret has resolved herself to stay behind in her home country, while maintaining her connections with Rhodes Island to act as the branch head in the capital of Kawalerielki. Despite Kazimierz being her country of birth, Margaret holds Rhodes Island as her home away from home dearly, and often returns for short stints of time to participate in operations that require her expertise.

Combat Prowess

Light-Based Originium Arts - While Margaret isn't Infected, her Elder Pegasian bloodline enables her to perform superhuman feats along with utilizing light itself as her main armament. Aptly named as the Radiant Knight for good reason, she's able to channel her searing arts into both weapons and armor, providing an astounding resilience to attacks along with maximized offensive potential.

- The light itself can manifest in physical form based on her current armaments, and can be wielded freely as an actual weapon if need be.

- While mainly meant for combat, Margaret's light can be used to help heal dire injuries and cauterize wounds, enabling her and her allies to maintain their positions for longer periods of time.

- There are many instances where Margaret's light is so immensely overwhelming, that it can effectively drown out or completely cancel other arts as a result.

Weapons Paragon - Due to her extensive training as a Knight, Margaret is capable of wielding all manner of weapons expertly. The most notable are her bladed mace & shield, along with a swordspear that she can detach for versatility in close quarters.

- The mace is capable of acting as a focus for Margaret's arts, magnifying her range and reach by several times at full strength. This works particularly well for healing others as necessary.

- Margaret's armor, granted to her by Rhodes Island, has been extensively modified by the Head of Rhodes Island's Engineering Department known as Closure. This in conjunction with her shield provides the Knight with resilience beyond normal measures.

- Based off her grandfather's fighting style, Margaret originally dedicated herself to mastering the swordspear, and wields the uniquely modified armament with deft precision. When using the polearm, her arts become tremendously destructive, trading defense for offense.
