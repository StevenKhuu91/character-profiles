-- Story --

A former Knight of Kazimierz, Margaret once held an illustrious position and title in her homeland, regarded often as the Radiant Knight due to how her light-based arts was utilized. An incident with the many bureaucrats and wealthy nobles there however spiraled her down to the rocky path of defamation, as she was an outspoken proponent who rallied against the state of Kazimierz today. Rumors turned into truth, and sure enough Margaret was outed to be an Infected, one of the multitudes of shunned who contracted Oripathy. Exiled from her homeland, Margaret came across coincidental but fateful allies in the form of Shining and Nightingale, two Sarkaz women who joined with the Radiant Knight to form 'The Followers'. As a vigilante band who did whatever they could to combat the injustices against the Infected, they earned the deserved attention of a certain pharmaceutical company by the name of Rhodes Island, and the rest was history.
